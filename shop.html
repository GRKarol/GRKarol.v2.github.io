The story behind it

Photo

Karol

Founder, Grankers Team

I created Progressio because I had this problem myself. I'd read dozens of self-improvement books, changed my mindset, but never found one product that brought it all together into a working system for an entire year.

One day I saw an ad on TikTok — a simple journal with checkboxes. Did you drink water? Check. What are you grateful for? Check. I thought: I can build something better.

I started in Canva in 2024. The first version was worthless — I threw it out because I'd have felt guilty selling it to anyone. The second was better. The third is the Progressio you hold now.

I didn't create this for a business. I created it because I needed it myself — and I believed others had exactly the same problem.

Grankers Team · Warsaw, Poland
