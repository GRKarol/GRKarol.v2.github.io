Pactum Progressio

I, fully aware of my decision, take my oath today
before the only judge who matters – the man I intend to become.

I vow to keep my word to myself, to turn excuses
into discipline, and intentions into daily action.

I swear to carry out the plans I create on these pages.

I understand that this journal is not just a place to write,
but a contract I am making with my future.

I swear to treat this journal as a witness to my transformation.

I know that its strength lies not in the perfection of each entry, but in its consistency
and courage to continue working, even after stumbling.

I know that abandoning this pact is a tacit consent to stagnation.

By signing, I choose to fight. I choose to grow. I choose to take full responsibility.
